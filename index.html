Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is included in my conference ticket?

Your ticket grants you full access to all keynote sessions, breakout workshops, the sponsor expo hall, and networking events (lunches and evening socials included).

2. Is this conference suitable for beginners?

Yes! We curate specific "Foundations" tracks and workshops designed for developers new to the field. Look for the "Beginner" tag on our schedule.

3. What if I cannot attend for all three days?

We do not offer single-day passes at this time. Your ticket is for the full three-day experience to ensure you get the most value and networking opportunities.

4. Do you offer group or student discounts?

Yes. We have special rates for groups of 5+ and a limited number of heavily discounted student passes. Please contact our sales team for details.

5. What is the venue like, and is it accessible?

The conference is held at the [Convention Center Name]. It is fully ADA compliant. We will provide a detailed accessibility map and quiet room information closer to the event date.

6. What is the refund policy?

Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. Cancellations within 30 days are not eligible for refunds, but you may transfer your ticket to another person.
